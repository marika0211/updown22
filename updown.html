Welcome to Up&Down

The mission of the Flip Fly trampoline center is to bring joy, fun and laughter into your life, to give you a boost of energy, to give you a wonderful state of health and vigor.

Our only goal is your good health, the acquisition of good habits, the development of physical skills and care for the figure.

We conceived our trampoline centers as a place for joint leisure activities with a child, because jumping on a trampoline is equally fun for both children and adults. It is no secret that joint exercises on a trampoline make it easy to establish a special emotional connection between a parent and a child, due to the emergence of conscientious hobbies.

More about us

With us you can attend specially designed classes with a coach or jump on your own.
Free jumps can be performed under the supervision of a trainer.
At the entrance to the trampoline arena you will definitely have an easy warm-up and the necessary instructions.
We have developed more than a dozen areas of sports training for group classes, personal or family.
Choose the most convenient training system for you, see the work schedule or find out how much this or that program costs in the relevant sections of the site or on the Prices page.

Conditions for comfortable activities and rest: personal lockers for clothes, changing rooms with showers, a cafe.

Safe equipment: all trampolines are fenced with soft mats.

Professionalism: all trainers of the trampoline club received special sports training and first aid training.

Taking care of each guest: the coach conducts compulsory warm-ups, briefings and basic trampoline training.
